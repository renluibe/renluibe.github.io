Biography个人简介

Long Ren is a Full Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, University of International Business and Economics. His research focuses on management science, operations management, supply chain finance, risk management, business intelligence, and AI-enabled managerial decision making.

任龙，对外经济贸易大学人工智能与数据科学学院长聘教授、博士生导师，惠园优秀青年学者。研究方向主要包括管理科学、运营管理、供应链金融、风险管理、商务智能与人工智能赋能的管理决策。

“Zwei Dinge erfüllen das Gemüt mit immer neuer und zunehmender Bewunderung und Ehrfurcht, je öfter und anhaltender sich das Nachdenken damit beschäftigt: der bestirnte Himmel über mir und das moralische Gesetz in mir.”

Professor Ren received his Ph.D. in Management Science from the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Professor Jian Chen. He has been a visiting scholar at Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis, and a visiting professor at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology Business School.

He has published more than 40 papers and articles in leading international and Chinese journals and major media outlets, including UTD 24, FT 50, ABS 4-star and CCF-listed journals. Several papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited Papers.

任龙毕业于清华大学经济管理学院管理科学与工程专业，获管理科学博士学位，导师为陈剑教授。曾在圣路易斯华盛顿大学奥林商学院访问学习与研究，合作导师包括 Prof. Lingxiu Dong 与 Prof. Dennis Zhang；曾任香港科技大学商学院访问教授，合作导师为 Prof. Man Yu。

近年以第一作者或合作者身份在 UTD 24、FT 50、ABS 4 星与 CCF 列表期刊等国内外高水平期刊及主流媒体发表论文 40 多篇，多篇入选 ESI 高被引论文。相关研究覆盖运营管理、供应链金融、平台经济、金融科技、风险管理、偏好学习、强化学习与企业数智化转型等领域。

Research Interests研究方向

  • Digital and intelligent operations management, including platform operations, supply chain finance, sustainable operations, and operations-marketing-information systems interface.
  • Business intelligence and intelligent decision making, including preference learning, reinforcement learning, online optimization, and data-driven managerial models.
  • FinTech and risk management, including enterprise risk management, platform financing, operational and financial hedging, value-at-risk models, and robo-advisory systems.
  • AI and digital transformation of firms, including applications of artificial intelligence, big data, blockchain, and intelligent optimization in management practice.
  • 数智驱动的企业运营管理：平台运营管理、供应链金融、运营—营销—信息系统交叉研究与可持续运营管理。
  • 商务智能与智能决策：智能决策、偏好学习、强化学习、在线优化与数据驱动的管理模型。
  • 金融科技与风险管理：全面风险管理、平台融资、运营与金融对冲、风险价值模型与智能投顾。
  • 企业数智化转型与管理：人工智能、大数据、区块链与智能优化技术在企业管理中的应用。
