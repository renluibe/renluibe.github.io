Biography个人简介

Long Ren is a Full Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, University of International Business and Economics. His research lies at the intersection of management science, operations management, business intelligence, FinTech, risk management, platform economy, and AI-enabled managerial decision making.

任龙，对外经济贸易大学人工智能与数据科学学院长聘教授、博士生导师，惠园优秀青年学者。研究方向主要包括管理科学、运营管理、商务智能、金融科技、风险管理、平台经济与人工智能赋能的管理决策。

“Zwei Dinge erfüllen das Gemüt mit immer neuer und zunehmender Bewunderung und Ehrfurcht, je öfter und anhaltender sich das Nachdenken damit beschäftigt: der bestirnte Himmel über mir und das moralische Gesetz in mir.”

任龙，对外经济贸易大学人工智能与数据科学学院长聘教授、博士生导师，惠园优秀青年学者，清华大学经济管理学院管理科学博士（导师：陈剑教授），圣路易斯华盛顿大学奥林商学院访问学者（Advisor: Prof. Lingxiu Dong; Prof. Dennis Zhang）、香港科技大学商学院访问教授（Advisor: Prof. Man Yu）。

近年以第一作者或合作者身份在 UTD 24、FT 50、ABS 4 星与 CCF 列表期刊等国内外高水平期刊与三报一刊等主流媒体发表论文 40 多篇（多篇入选 ESI 高被引论文），包括《管理工程学报》《中国管理科学》《中国人口·资源与环境》《光明日报》, Annals of Operations Research, European Journal of Operational Research, IEEE TRANS, Information & Management, Information Processing & Management, International Journal of Production Economics, International Journal of Electronic Commerce, Production and Operations Management, Reliability Engineering & System Safety, Transportation Research 等。

另有多篇工作论文目前在 UTD 24 期刊深轮次修改阶段，并先后多次受邀在管理科学国际顶级会议，如 INFORMS 年会、M&SOM SIG 年会以及 POMS 年会等作报告。共主持国家省部级纵向项目 5 项，参与国家自然科学基金重大、重点项目，及中国石油、米其林、IBM、杉树科技等企业横向课题多项。

曾荣获中国物流与采购联合会科技进步奖，多次获得国家一级学会优秀论文奖。相关研究被美国工程院、中国科学院、中国工程院院士团队正面引用与评价，并在众安银行、京投公司与中国石油获得实际应用并产生良好社会效益。

出版教材两部：《人文社科人工智能素养与实践》（高等教育出版社）、《AI时代的Python编程入门》（清华大学出版社）；拟出版“AI+管理”系列教材《运营与供应链管理：AI+实践》（清华大学出版社），拟出版专著《数智驱动的智能决策理论与应用》。

Professor Ren received his Ph.D. in Management Science from the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Professor Jian Chen. He has been a visiting scholar at Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis (Advisors: Prof. Lingxiu Dong and Prof. Dennis Zhang), and a visiting professor at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology Business School (Advisor: Prof. Man Yu).

He has published more than 40 papers and articles as first author or coauthor in leading international and Chinese journals, including journals on the UTD 24, FT 50, ABS 4-star and CCF lists, as well as major Chinese media outlets. His publications include papers in Production and Operations Management, European Journal of Operational Research, IEEE Transactions journals, Information & Management, Information Processing & Management, International Journal of Production Economics, International Journal of Electronic Commerce, Reliability Engineering & System Safety, Transportation Research, Annals of Operations Research, and leading Chinese journals such as Journal of Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management, Chinese Journal of Management Science, and China Population, Resources and Environment. Several papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited Papers.

Several of his working papers are currently under advanced-round revision at UTD 24 journals. He has been invited to present his research at leading international conferences in management science, including INFORMS Annual Meeting, M&SOM SIG Meeting, and POMS Annual Conference. He has served as PI for five national or provincial-level research grants and participated in major and key projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, as well as industry projects with PetroChina, Michelin, IBM, and Sumscope.

His research has received the Science and Technology Progress Award from the China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing and several best-paper awards from national academic societies. His work has been positively cited and evaluated by academic teams led by members of the U.S. National Academy of Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chinese Academy of Engineering, and has been applied in ZA Bank, Beijing Infrastructure Investment, and PetroChina.

He has published two textbooks, AI Literacy and Practice for Humanities and Social Sciences and Introduction to Python Programming in the AI Era. He is preparing an AI+Management textbook, Operations and Supply Chain Management: AI+Practice, and a monograph, Data-Intelligence-Driven Intelligent Decision Theory and Applications.

Research Interests研究方向

  • 数智驱动的企业运营管理研究：平台运营管理、供应链金融、运营、营销与信息系统交叉研究、可持续运营管理。
  • 商务智能与智能决策：智能决策、偏好学习、强化学习、在线优化。
  • 金融科技与风险管理：全面风险管理、平台融资、运营与金融对冲、智能投顾。
  • 企业数智化转型与管理：人工智能、大数据、区块链与智能优化技术在企业管理中的应用。
  • Digital and intelligent operations management: platform operations, supply chain finance, operations-marketing-information systems interface, and sustainable operations.
  • Business intelligence and intelligent decision making: preference learning, reinforcement learning, online optimization, and data-driven managerial models.
  • FinTech and risk management: enterprise risk management, platform financing, operational and financial hedging, and robo-advisory systems.
  • AI and digital transformation of firms: managerial applications of artificial intelligence, big data, blockchain, and intelligent optimization.
